The data include a nexus tree with all species in OthoMaM as well as fasta files of alignments of the following genes (all downloaded from the OrthoMaM database)


### control0
5 randomly selected genes based on their different number of species and alignment length (only used for initial test of time consumption):

*AGRP,  IQCF1,  KLRK,  PCDHAC2,  PDE4D*


### control1
3 pluripotency markers:

*POU5F1,	SOX2, 	NANOG*

*ACTR1A,	DDIT4,	DOOST,	EHF,	EVPL,	GAS7,	MAP1B,	MRPL27,	MYO1A,	NPAS4,	PAFAH1B3,  PAQR3,	PHF24,	POLR1C,	QRICH1,	TGFB3,	TGM1,	USH1C,	ZC3HC1,	ZCCHC17*

*IGF1,	IGF2,	IGF1R,	IGF2R,	IGFBP2,	IGFBP4,	IGFBP5,	PAPPA,	PAPPA2,	STC1,	STC2*
